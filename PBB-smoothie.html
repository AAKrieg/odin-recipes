Peanut Butter Banana Smoothie

A peanut butter and banana smoothie is a refreshing drink for the mornings. I've heard it's good for detoxing but I only beleive in crystals so who knows.

This sweet, satly, and tasty drink will be the beverage you'll wake up wanting for every morning.

Ingredients

  • 2 bananas, broken into chunks
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons honey, or to taste
  • 2 cups ice cubes

Steps

  1. Optional: Freeze bananas beforehand
  2. Place bananas, milk, peanut butter, honey, and ice cubes in a blender; blend until smooth, about 30 seconds.
